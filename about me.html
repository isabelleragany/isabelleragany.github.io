About Me

I am a meticulous student in my fourth year of Integrated Biomedical Engineering and Health Sciences, specializing in Electrical Engineering. Outside of academics, I received my Private Pilots License and Glider Pilots License. Being a pilot has taught me the importance of dedication and attention to detail, which are traits I apply to each design.

Design aside, I also am passionate about research. I have been a research assistant for several months. The projects I developed this summer include software development through C and Python to interface with a multichannel potentiostat to automate data collection and streaming. I also worked on a project to find the optimal values for several variables in Hot Strip Mill Model software. Specific mechanical properties based on known steel chemistries and rolling schedules were identified during this project.
